Pandas are in danger

June 30 ,2020 by Abdullah



Little Gambino The Panda has lost the will to live, he suffers from severe depression .
Due to living in far places and the number of the pandas has decreased dramatically during the past 3 years.


Burrito "Gambino's brother" has lost his brother from 2 years and they could not find each other since then, some fellows attacked them back then and they ran in different ways back then.

But luckily, people at the forest of lumbardo found Burrito and they noticed that he has the same birth sign as the panda they found earlier in cocona forests so they brought the brothers back together.


And now they live happily in the lumbardo forrests.
